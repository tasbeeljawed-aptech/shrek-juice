Our Story

Fresh Juice started with a simple dream to bring nature’s best directly to your glass. We wanted to create a healthier alternative to sugary drinks by focusing on freshness, nutrition, and authentic flavors.

From hand-picked fruits sourced from local farms to eco-friendly packaging, every step of our journey is guided by one principle — keeping it natural and fresh.

About Us

Shrek Juice brings you 100% fresh & healthy juices, smoothies, and shakes. Our mission is to keep you refreshed naturally.

Shrek Juice is more than just a juice bar — it’s a lifestyle choice for those who value health, freshness, and great taste. Since our beginning, we have been committed to serving 100% natural juices, smoothies, protein shakes, and mocktails made from hand-picked fruits and vegetables.
